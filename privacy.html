Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

NFC/Code Smart Attendance PWA is a classroom attendance tracking application. We are committed to protecting user privacy.

Data Collection

The app collects the following data for attendance tracking purposes only:

  • Student names (uploaded by professor)
  • Last 4 digits of student numbers (uploaded by professor)
  • NFC card serial numbers (with student consent)
  • QR code content (when scanned for attendance)
  • Attendance timestamps

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the device only. No data is transmitted to any server or third party. The app works entirely offline after installation.

Data Security

  • NFC serial numbers are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage
  • Professor authentication (NFC card or password) is required for administrative actions
  • Backup files are encrypted with a user-provided passphrase

Consent

Students are asked for explicit consent before their NFC card serial number is recorded. Consent is timestamped and recorded.

Data Export

Attendance data can be exported as CSV files or Blackboard-compatible format. Exported files are saved to the device and may leave the device when shared by the user via Bluetooth, email, or other sharing methods.

Data Deletion

The professor can delete all data at any time using the App Reset function. Individual course data can be removed via the menu. Students can request removal of their data from the professor.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data leaves the device automatically.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by educators and adult students (18+). It is not directed at children under 13.
